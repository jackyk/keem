My Story

I’m Jacky and I’m 22. I’m a self-taught web developer, a technology enthusiast and an aspiring hacker. I began my journey into tech a year ago and now teach at Tunapanda Institute in Kibera, Nairobi.

Before I got a computer of my own, I would frequent cyber cafes. More often than not, I would be the only girl there. It is rare to find girls associating themselves with computer science. Providing a safe space and mentorship for girls to learn about technology can break down prejudice they have towards tech and boost their motivation to learn more.
